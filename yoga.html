What is Yoga?

Yoga is an ancient Indian practice that unites body, mind, and breath. It improves flexibility, strength, and inner peace.

What is Meditation?

Meditation is the practice of calming the mind by focusing on breathing or awareness. It reduces stress and increases clarity.
